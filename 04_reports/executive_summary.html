üî¨ Methodology Overview

Our analysis employs a comprehensive event study framework with multiple factor models and policy interaction specifications:

Event Study Model:
$$CAR_{i,[t_1,t_2]} = \sum_{t=t_1}^{t_2} AR_{i,t} = \sum_{t=t_1}^{t_2} (R_{i,t} - E[R_{i,t}|X_t])$$
Policy Interaction Model:
$$CAR_{i,t} = \alpha + \beta_1 ESG\_Change_{i,t} + \beta_2 Policy_{s,t} + \beta_3 (ESG\_Change_{i,t} \times Policy_{s,t}) + \gamma X_{i,t-1} + \epsilon_{i,t}$$
Carhart 4-Factor Model:
$$R_{i,t} - R_{f,t} = \alpha_i + \beta_1 MKT_t + \beta_2 SMB_t + \beta_3 HML_t + \beta_4 UMD_t + \epsilon_{i,t}$$
